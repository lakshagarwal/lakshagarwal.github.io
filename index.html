Hi! I’m Laksh, the co-founder of Ednius, an AI-powered grading platform designed to transform education by providing instant, personalized feedback to students. We’re addressing one of the biggest challenges in education: the slow, manual grading process. Our platform enables faster, more insightful assessments, helping educators save time while improving student outcomes.

Beyond Ednius, I contribute to research at Simon Fraser University's OPA Lab, focusing on enhancing digital trust and data accuracy. This work fuels my interest in building reliable, scalable systems that people can trust in our increasingly connected world.

With a background in Computing Science and a Certificate in Innovation and Entrepreneurship from Simon Fraser University, I bring both technical expertise and a drive to innovate. I’m passionate about solving real-world problems with technology and always looking to connect with others who share that vision.

My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of AI and machine learning, not just in education but across industries that can benefit from more efficient and intelligent systems. I believe that by integrating cutting-edge technology into everyday processes, we can create a more seamless and impactful world.
